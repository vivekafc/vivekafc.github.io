WHAT IS BLOOMWORKS?

Bloomworks is a generative art and sound bot inspired by James Joyce's character, Molly Bloom, from the book Ulysses. In this book, Bloom performs a soliloquy as she lies in bed next to her husband, and goes on to say one of the longest sentences in literature history, clocking in at 3,687 words. The twitter bot takes this sentence and breaks it into randomized phrases. The values of the waveform of these phrases is then used to generate the values by which the art is generated. Clicking the artwork will playback the phrase on which it's based.

Made with the help of p5.js.
