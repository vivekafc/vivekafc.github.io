A Day in the Life is my attempt at an immersive experience on the web. Following the format of a Choose Your Own Adventure type game, this experience aims to convey to the user my experiences with having insomnia at the New School, and how it made one class in particular extremely tough for me, as my relationship with my teacher deteriorated.

I have found my experience with insomnia to be really hard, as I tend to miss a lot of morning classes from the feeling of illness that came with it from a lack of sleep. Further more, due to my absences, I developed a feeling that many of my teachers didn't appreciate me as a student and this made me even more anxious, thus worsening my insomnia.

Designed and developed by Vivek Bajaj, for Immersive Storytelling, Fall 2019.
