Dragon Ball Z is about spiritual enlightenment Moving from a place of ignorance toward a place of insight and learning to harmonize and progress as a group, drawing insiration from Mahayana Buddhism The anime and manga itself is based off of an Ancient Chinese Novel, ‘The Journey to the West’

This website aims to explore the more philosphical aspects of the show and to give us a more in-depth look into how each character portrays a part of us and what we can learn from them.
