Goku is often regarded a poor father to Gohan. However, he loves him more than anyone else. One thing to be taken into account is that the man is Japanese, so his perspectives on family surely would be somewhat different. Commonly in Asian cultures even today, a family is dependent of one’s standing in society.

Because “family” is treated as a part of oneself, that is considered arrogant boasting. A humble man, say when his wife is praised, likely would answer “No, she’s nothing special. I am nothing special.”
