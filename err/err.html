VIVEK BAJAJ

During my second semester at Parsons, I developed the East River Raiders F.C. project.

Soccer has always been a huge passion of mine and is one of the reasons I began creating art with real passion. I decided to create a fictional MLS team called the East River Raiders.

For it, I developed a crest, designed custom home and away jerseys, as well as made a mockup of a potential iOS/Android app.
